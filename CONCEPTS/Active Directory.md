#CONCEPTS 



A **forest** in an AD its the top-level container that holds domains, users, groups and resources. Each forest can have its own boundary. 



# Relations of trust

A **trust** is a link between two forest or domains to allow users to access resources in the other. 

* **two-way forest trust**: is a mutual relationship between two Active Directories forest that allows users and resources in one forest to be authenticated and access resources in the other forests and vicevers.
