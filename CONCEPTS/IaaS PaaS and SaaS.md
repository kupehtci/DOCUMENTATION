#CONCEPTS #CLOUD

# IaaS

IaaS or Infrastructure as a Service provides a raw infrastructure resources such as VMs, storage or networking via the cloud. 

# PaaS

PaaS or Platform as a Service offers a managed platform for building, deploying and maintaining applications without worrying about the underlying infrastructure

# SaaS

SaaS or Software as a Service delivers a ready-to-use application over the internet that don't require either installation or maintenance by the user. 

Its fully maintained by the proprietary company (SaaS Provider).
