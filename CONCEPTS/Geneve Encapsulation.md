# Geneve Encapsulation

**Geneve Encapsulation** or **Ge**neric **Ne**twork **V**irtualization **E**ncapsulation is an standard tunnel format that encapsulates packets inside an UDP packet to create virtual networks under an IP infrastructure. 

Encapsulates the packet as a **payload** with a header and carries it over UDP or IPv4 / IPv6 enabling underlaying virtual networks within a physical network. 


Its widely used for network virtualization or to send packets to other devices without altering the original packet data.
