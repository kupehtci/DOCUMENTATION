# Linux Distros

Distro or distribution 


Its more recommended to choose between this distros depending on the use cases: 

The main distros are: 
* **Ubuntu**: the most popular with lots of documentation and guides and lots of packages available. 
* **Debian**: very stable and very recommended for servers. 
* **Fedora**: used normally by develoopers
* **Linux Mint**: based in ubuntu and debian very orientes into the desktop and ease of use. One of the best options to migrate from windows. 

More advanced distros for users that want more control or low level configuration: 

* Arch linux
* Gentoo
* openSUSE


Orientes for bussines and companies: 
* Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) is more typical for companies and servers. 
* Ubuntu Server: used commonly in Cloud with a huge ecosystem of packages. 
* SUSE Linux Enterprise: more oriented in administration tools.
