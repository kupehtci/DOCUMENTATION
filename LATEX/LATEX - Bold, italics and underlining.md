#LATEX 

### Text modifiers

In LaTeX exists three main modifiers of the text weight or decoration: 

* **Bold:** `\textbf{}`
* **Italics:** `\textit{}`
* **Underlined:** `\underline{}`

```LATEX
Some of the \textbf{greatest} 
discoveries in \underline{science} 
were made by \textbf{\textit{accident}}.
```
