# Security

The **security** in terms of software quality, its the protection capacity of the information and the application data in a format that only the authorized users and products has correspondent access depending their authorization level. 

Also is related with the ability of a system of defense from malicious attacks. 

The sub-characteristics from this quality aspect are: 
* **Confidentiality**: the data is only accesible from the users that are authorized to do so. 
* **Integrity**: guarantee the status of the system and the data is protected from malicious modifications and non authorized or error modifications. 
* **Non repudiation**: be able to ensure that the actions has taken place by who. 
* **Authenticity**: capacity of a system to ensure that the identity of a subject or resource is true. 
* **Resistance**: be able to maintain the status of the software under some malicious conditions or under an attack.
