# DevOps

DevOps or Development and Operations is like a bridge between Operations (Systems or TI) and Development. 

Its main purposes or topics are: 
* Automation: implementing automation for a faster delivery of the applications. 
* Tooling: implementation of new tools and toolchains
* Practices: 
	* Implementing elements of Agile and IT service management to improve efficiency and workflows. 

DevOps encourages a culture of collaboration to deliver business value faster and cheaper
